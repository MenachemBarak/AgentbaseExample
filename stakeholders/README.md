# Stakeholders

The **stakeholders** folder maps the people and parties DriveEasy's agents act *for*
and *with* — everyone whose interests shape a decision but who is not necessarily a
role in the org chart. Entries are split three ways, and `graph.md` records the
relationships between them.

## self/
- The DriveEasy base itself and its agentic workforce — the West Region branch agents
  acting on the company's behalf.

## internal/
- **Elena Reyes** — CEO, DriveEasy Car Rental (ultimate owner of vision & mission).
- Operational leadership already in the org chart: Marcus Hale, James Carter,
  Priya Patel, and Aisha Khan — consulted on policy and escalations.

## external/
- **Corporate-account clients** — companies with negotiated fleet rates and monthly
  invoicing.
- **Insurance partners** — underwrite damage claims; gated into the return-lot
  damage-review workflow.
- **Vehicle suppliers / dealerships** — sell and buy back fleet vehicles.
- **Airport authorities** — landlords of the airport counters and shuttle lanes.

## graph.md
Captures who influences whom (e.g. insurance partner -> damage review -> corporate
client invoice) so agents can trace stakeholder impact before acting.

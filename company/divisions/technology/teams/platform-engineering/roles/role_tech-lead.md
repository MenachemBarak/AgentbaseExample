# Role — Tech Lead (role_tech-lead)

## Definition
The **Tech Lead** runs the **Platform Engineering** team day to day and leads the
**bug-solving squad**. This role is held by **Omar Haddad**, reporting to CTO
**Nadia Brandt** and directing the frontend, backend, and QA engineers.

## Responsibilities
- Triage incoming bugs and features; decide what is worked, by whom, and when.
- Lead the bug-solving squad: set the goal, assign the specialist agents to the
  engineering roles, and approve the fix that ships.
- Own release decisions and command Sev-1 incident response on the platform repos.
- Keep the team's standards — tests, environments, review — enforced.

## Purpose
Omar exists to make sure every bug and feature has a clear owner and a proven
outcome — turning a reported problem into a verified, shipped change without
breaking the platform the company rents cars on.

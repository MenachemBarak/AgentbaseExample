# Technology Division — Teams

The **Technology** division delivers through a single team today.

## Platform Engineering
Owns DriveEasy's platform code end to end — the `booking-system`, `pricing-engine`,
`counter-terminal`, and `fleet-telematics` repos. It ships features and fixes
production bugs, and runs the **bug-solving squad** declared under
`agentic_bridge/driveeasy_bridge/bridge/bug-solving/`. Led by Tech Lead
**Omar Haddad** under CTO **Nadia Brandt**. See
[platform-engineering/team.md](platform-engineering/team.md).

## Boundaries
Platform Engineering owns *how the software is built and repaired*. It does **not**
own *what the software should do for customers* — that is Customer Experience — nor
the pricing economics it implements, which belong to Finance & Administration. As
the division grows, future teams (e.g. Data/Telematics, Infrastructure) would sit
alongside Platform Engineering here.

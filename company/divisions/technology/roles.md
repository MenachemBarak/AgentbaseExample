# Roles — Technology Division

## Role hierarchy
The Technology division is led by one role at the division level:

- **role_cto** — **Nadia Brandt**, Chief Technology Officer. Owns all of DriveEasy's
  software: the platform repos, engineering staffing, and the agentic transformation
  of how the platform is built and maintained. An active code contributor herself.

The CTO sits above the **Platform Engineering** team (see
[teams/platform-engineering/roles.md](teams/platform-engineering/roles.md)) and
reports up to CEO **Elena Reyes**.

- [roles/role_cto.md](roles/role_cto.md)

## Collaboration & communication
- **Cross-division:** Nadia partners with Customer Experience (booking journey),
  Finance & Administration (pricing-engine economics), and Fleet Management
  (telematics) — they define *what* the platform must do; Technology owns *how* it
  is built and kept healthy.
- **Incident path:** production bugs surface from any division or branch, land with
  Platform Engineering, and are worked by the **bug-solving squad** declared under
  `agentic_bridge/driveeasy_bridge/bridge/bug-solving/`.
- **Escalation:** Sev-1 incidents and major release decisions go Tech Lead -> CTO;
  budget and cross-division priorities go CTO -> CEO.

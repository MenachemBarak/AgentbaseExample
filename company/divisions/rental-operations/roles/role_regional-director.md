# Role: Director of Regional Operations

## Definition
The **Director of Regional Operations** is a division-level leadership role in
**Rental Operations** at **DriveEasy Car Rental**, held by **Dana Okonkwo**.
Dana reports to **Marcus Hale**, VP of Rental Operations, and oversees the
division's three regional organizations — **West**, **East**, and **Central
Region Operations** — coaching the regional managers (such as **James Carter**
in the West) who run them.

## Responsibilities
- Oversee the regional organizations and keep their operations consistent with
  division standards.
- Coach and hold accountable the regional managers, and help them balance
  staffing and vehicles across regions with their fleet coordinators.
- Monitor regional performance — utilization, wait times, returns accuracy, and
  customer satisfaction — and drive improvement wherever a region lags.
- Roll regional issues, risks, and results up to Marcus Hale, and cascade
  division decisions back down to the regions.
- Lead the weekly regional managers' call and standardize what works in one
  region across the others.

## Purpose
To turn the division's standards into reliable, on-the-ground execution in every
region — so a customer gets the same fast, friendly, fairly-priced rental
whether they pick up in the West, East, or Central footprint.

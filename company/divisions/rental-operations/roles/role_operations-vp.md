# Role: VP of Rental Operations

## Definition
The **VP of Rental Operations** is the head of the **Rental Operations** division
of **DriveEasy Car Rental**, held by **Marcus Hale**. It is the most senior
operations role in the company, reporting directly to CEO **Elena Reyes** and
sitting alongside the heads of Fleet Management, Customer Experience, and
Finance & Administration. Marcus owns all branch operations and directly
oversees **Dana Okonkwo**, Director of Regional Operations.

## Responsibilities
- Own end-to-end branch operations across every region and the full rental
  lifecycle: reserve -> pickup -> in-use -> return -> invoice.
- Set operating standards, staffing models, and performance targets
  (utilization, wait time, customer satisfaction) for all branches.
- Own the division's operating budget and headcount.
- Represent Rental Operations to the CEO and negotiate commitments with peer
  divisions — vehicle supply with Fleet Management, pricing and billing with
  Finance & Administration, service standards with Customer Experience.
- Hold the Director of Regional Operations accountable for consistent regional
  execution.

## Purpose
To make sure DriveEasy can reliably put customers on the road at scale — that
every branch, in every region, runs to one high standard and delivers on the
company's promise of effortless mobility.

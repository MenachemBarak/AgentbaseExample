# Role: Regional Fleet Coordinator (West)

## Definition
**Sofia Marin** is the Regional Fleet Coordinator for **West Region Operations**, within the
Rental Operations division of DriveEasy Car Rental. Sofia reports to the West Regional Manager,
**James Carter**, and works across all West groups and branches to keep vehicles where demand
needs them.

## Responsibilities
- Balance the vehicle mix (economy, compact, SUV, luxury, van) across West branches.
- Plan and schedule vehicle transfers between branches to cover surges and one-way rentals.
- Monitor GPS telematics — location, mileage, fuel, diagnostics — to flag cars needing service or repositioning.
- Coordinate with the Fleet Management division on additions, recalls, and retirements.
- Give James a clear daily picture of fleet availability and gaps.

## Purpose
To make sure the right vehicles are in the right branches at the right time, so no West
customer is turned away and no car sits idle when another branch needs it.

# Role: Regional Manager (West)

## Definition
**James Carter** is the West Regional Manager — the top role in **West Region Operations**,
an organization within the Rental Operations division of DriveEasy Car Rental. James reports
to **Dana Okonkwo** (Director of Regional Operations) and leads every group and branch in the
West. The Regional Fleet Coordinator, **Sofia Marin**, reports to him.

## Responsibilities
- Own the West's results: utilization, revenue, service scores, and staffing.
- Set targets and priorities for the Airport, City, and Suburban Branches Groups.
- Approve regional pricing guidance, promotions, and major staffing decisions.
- Partner with Sofia to keep vehicles balanced across branches as demand shifts.
- Escalate region-wide risks (fleet shortages, system outages) to the division.

## Purpose
To turn division strategy into on-the-ground rentals across the West — making sure every
western branch can put customers in a reliable, fairly-priced vehicle, quickly.

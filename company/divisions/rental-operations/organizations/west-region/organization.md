# West Region Operations

## Overview
West Region Operations is one of three regional organizations inside the
**Rental Operations** division of DriveEasy Car Rental. It runs every DriveEasy
branch across the western states — airport, downtown, and suburban locations —
turning division strategy into day-to-day rentals on the ground.

The region is led by **James Carter** (West Regional Manager), with **Sofia Marin**
(Regional Fleet Coordinator) keeping the right vehicles in the right branches. The
region reports up to **Dana Okonkwo**, Director of Regional Operations.

## How it works
West Region groups its branches by location type. Each group runs its own branches,
which staff the counters, prep vehicles, and process returns. The region:
- Sets staffing, service, and revenue targets for each group and branch.
- Uses the **booking system** and **pricing engine** to balance demand across the region.
- Moves vehicles between branches as bookings shift (e.g., weekend SUV demand at airports).
- Watches GPS telematics and the rental lifecycle: reserve -> pickup -> in-use -> return -> invoice.

## Purpose & Goals
- Hit regional utilization and revenue targets without sacrificing service quality.
- Keep every branch supplied with the right mix of economy, compact, SUV, luxury, and van vehicles.
- Resolve cross-branch issues (shortages, one-way rentals, overflow) quickly and fairly.
- Be the reliable western face of DriveEasy's promise of effortless mobility.

## Vision
A western footprint where any traveler, in any city, can pick up a ready vehicle and
drive away within minutes of arriving.

## Mission
Run the West's branches so customers get on the road quickly in a reliable,
fairly-priced vehicle, while vehicles and staff are deployed where demand needs them most.

# Airport Branches Group

## Overview
The **Airport Branches Group** runs every DriveEasy Car Rental branch located at
an airport inside **West Region Operations**. Airport branches share a distinct
rhythm — flight-driven demand spikes, terminal shuttle pickups, and a counter
that has to move travelers from baggage claim into the driver's seat fast. The
group brings those branches under one playbook so a customer landing at any West
airport gets the same quick, friendly handoff.

It is one of three groups in West Region Operations, sitting beside the City
Branches Group and the Suburban Branches Group. The group reports to West
Regional Manager **James Carter** and is led by Airport Branches Manager
**Priya Patel**.

## How it works
Priya sets targets and standards across the airport branches; Group Operations
Supervisor **Tom Becker** turns those into daily staffing, shuttle timing, and
counter coverage. The group leans on the shared **booking system** and **pricing
engine** so reservations, vehicle classes (economy, compact, SUV, luxury, van),
and rates stay consistent branch to branch.

Across the branch agents, deduped customer rental history and compressed GPS
telematics are shared once and reused everywhere — so a returning customer is
recognized whether they land at Metro, Harbor, or North County. The rental
lifecycle (reserve -> pickup -> in-use -> return -> invoice) runs the same way at
every airport counter.

## Purpose & goals
- Deliver a consistent, fast airport rental experience across all West airports.
- Match vehicle supply to flight-driven demand with Regional Fleet Coordinator
  **Sofia Marin**.
- Keep shuttle-to-counter-to-car wait times low during arrival peaks.
- Hold pricing, upgrade, and return standards steady across every branch.

## Vision & Mission
- **Vision:** make picking up a car at the airport as effortless as stepping off
  the plane.
- **Mission:** meet every arriving traveler with a ready vehicle, a fair price,
  and a friendly counter, so they leave the terminal on the road in minutes.

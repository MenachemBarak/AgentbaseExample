# Role: Group Manager — Airport Branches

## Definition
**Priya Patel** is the **Airport Branches Manager** for West Region Operations.
She sits at the top of the Airport Branches Group, reporting to West Regional
Manager **James Carter**. Below her is Group Operations Supervisor **Tom Becker**,
and through him the airport branch managers — including **Aisha Khan** at the
Metro Airport Branch.

## Responsibilities
- Own performance for all West airport branches: utilization, customer wait time,
  upgrade and return rates, and customer satisfaction.
- Set the airport branch playbook — counter standards, shuttle timing, and how
  the rental lifecycle is run consistently across branches.
- Plan for flight-driven demand with Regional Fleet Coordinator **Sofia Marin**,
  making sure the right vehicle classes are positioned ahead of arrival peaks.
- Direct and develop Tom Becker and the branch managers; resolve cross-branch
  conflicts over vehicles, staffing, and pricing exceptions.

## Purpose
Priya exists to make the airport rental experience effortless and consistent at
every West airport. She turns regional goals into branch reality, so a traveler
landing at any DriveEasy airport counter is on the road quickly, fairly priced,
and well treated.

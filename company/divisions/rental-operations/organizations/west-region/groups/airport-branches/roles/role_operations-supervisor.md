# Role: Operations Supervisor — Airport Branches

## Definition
**Tom Becker** is the **Group Operations Supervisor** for the Airport Branches
Group in West Region Operations. He reports to Airport Branches Manager **Priya
Patel** and works hand-in-hand with each airport branch manager (such as **Aisha
Khan** at the Metro Airport Branch) to keep daily operations running.

## Responsibilities
- Build daily and weekly staffing, shuttle, and counter-coverage plans across the
  airport branches to match flight schedules and demand.
- Monitor live operations — counter queues, shuttle turnaround, return-lot flow —
  and step in when arrival peaks or vehicle shortages threaten wait times.
- Coordinate vehicle moves between airport branches with Fleet Coordinator
  **Sofia Marin** when one terminal is short and another has surplus.
- Roll out Priya's standards on the ground and feed real branch data and
  exceptions back up so targets stay realistic.

## Purpose
Tom exists to turn the group's plan into smooth daily execution. He keeps people,
shuttles, and vehicles in the right place at the right time so each airport
counter can move arriving customers from terminal to driver's seat without delay.

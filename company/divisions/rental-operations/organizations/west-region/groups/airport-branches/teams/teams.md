# Teams — Airport Branches Group

The **Airport Branches Group** within **West Region Operations** runs the region's
airport rental locations. Each team below is one staffed branch with its own counter,
lot, and fleet, led by a Branch Manager who reports to Group Manager **Priya Patel**
and Operations Supervisor **Tom Becker**. The branches share the region's fleet pool
(balanced by Coordinator **Sofia Marin**) and DriveEasy's service standards, but each
owns the customers and vehicles at its own airport.

## Teams in this group

### Metro Airport Branch — *fully detailed here*
DriveEasy's flagship airport location at the main metro terminal, led by Branch
Manager **Aisha Khan**. Highest volume in the group, carrying all five vehicle
classes and three subteams (Rental Counter, Vehicle Prep, Returns & Inspection).
See [metro-airport-branch/team.md](metro-airport-branch/team.md).

### Harbor Airport Branch
Mid-size branch at the coastal harbor airport. Leisure- and cruise-heavy demand with
strong seasonal peaks; leans on SUVs and vans for groups and families.

### North County Airport Branch
Smaller regional-airport branch serving North County commuter and business travelers.
A compact fleet weighted toward economy and compact classes.

## Boundaries & relationships
- **Within the group:** the branches are peers; none manages another. Cars move
  between them only through Regional Fleet Coordinator **Sofia Marin**, never branch
  to branch.
- **Up:** all three report to the Airport Branches Group (Priya Patel / Tom Becker).
- **Sideways:** the City Branches Group and Suburban Branches Group run the region's
  non-airport locations; the airport teams own only their terminal operations.
- **Down:** each team contains its own subteams; the Metro Airport Branch's are
  detailed under metro-airport-branch/subteams/.

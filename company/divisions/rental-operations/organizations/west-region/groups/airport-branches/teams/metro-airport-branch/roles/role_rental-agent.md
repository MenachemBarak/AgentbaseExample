# Role — Rental Sales Agent (role_rental-agent)

## Definition
The **Rental Sales Agent** is the front-line, customer-facing role at a DriveEasy
branch. It is a generic, multi-seat role; at the **Metro Airport Branch** a typical
agent is **Diego Alvarez**. Agents report to Branch Manager **Aisha Khan** and take
day-to-day shift direction from Counter Team Lead **Grace Liu** on the Rental Counter.

## Responsibilities
- Greet arriving travelers and process pickups: verify the booking, license, and
  payment, then hand over the keys.
- Quote upgrades and add-ons from the **pricing engine** (vehicle class, insurance,
  GPS, child seats) and confirm the reservation in the booking system.
- Process returns at the desk, capturing mileage, fuel, and condition from each
  car's telematics, and start the invoice.
- Coordinate with Vehicle Prep and Returns & Inspection so a promised car is on the
  lot when the customer arrives.
- Raise pricing questions, damage findings, and fleet gaps to the Branch Manager.

## Purpose
The Rental Sales Agent is where DriveEasy meets the customer. Diego's job is to make
each reserve-to-invoice handoff quick, accurate, and friendly, so travelers leave
the counter on the road — and come back to DriveEasy next trip.

# Team — Metro Airport Branch

## Overview
The **Metro Airport Branch** is DriveEasy Car Rental's flagship rental location
within the **Airport Branches Group**, part of **West Region Operations**. Sitting
in the main terminal's ground-transportation hall and served by the DriveEasy
airport shuttle, it gets arriving travelers on the road quickly and takes returns
from departing ones. The branch is led by Branch Manager **Aisha Khan** and staffed
by a roster of Rental Sales Agents (exemplar **Diego Alvarez**).

## How it works
The branch turns each booking into a car on the road and back again, running the
rental lifecycle — **reserve -> pickup -> in-use -> return -> invoice** — across its
counter and lot. Counter terminals pull each reservation from the **booking
system**, the **pricing engine** quotes upgrades and add-ons, and GPS telematics
track every economy, compact, SUV, luxury, and van on the lot. Work flows across
three subteams: the **Rental Counter** checks customers in and out, **Vehicle Prep**
readies clean, fueled cars, and **Returns & Inspection** closes out each rental.
Aisha sets the daily plan with Group Operations Supervisor **Tom Becker** and draws
cars from Regional Fleet Coordinator **Sofia Marin**.

## Purpose & goals
- Deliver fast, friendly pickups and returns that keep airport travelers moving.
- Keep the right mix of vehicle classes ready so no confirmed booking goes unfilled.
- Hit the utilization, turnaround, and customer-satisfaction targets set by the
  Airport Branches Group.
- Escalate fleet shortfalls, damage disputes, and staffing gaps to group leadership
  early, before they reach the customer.

This team is delivered by three subteams — Rental Counter, Vehicle Prep, and

# Role: Rental Counter Associate (role_rental-associate)

## Definition
**Ben Foster** is a **Rental Counter Associate** in the Rental Counter subteam at
the Metro Airport Branch (Airport Branches Group, West Region Operations, Rental
Operations division of DriveEasy Car Rental). He works the desk under Counter
Team Lead **Grace Liu**.

## Responsibilities
- Greet arriving customers — many off the airport shuttle — and pull their
  reservation from the **booking system** on the counter terminal.
- Verify driver's license and payment method, and confirm the booked vehicle
  class (economy, compact, SUV, luxury, van).
- Offer upgrades, extras, and protection products priced by the **pricing
  engine**, then issue keys and the signed rental agreement for the pickup.
- Direct customers to their staged vehicle, and at drop-off pass them to Returns
  & Inspection.
- Log each interaction; rely on **headroom**-compressed, deduped customer history
  to see prior rentals at a glance instead of re-reading long transcripts.
- Escalate declined cards, ID issues, and disputes to Grace Liu.

## Purpose
Ben exists to give each customer a fast, friendly, accurate check-in and
check-out — the human moment that turns a booking into a car on the road and a
return into a clean close-out.

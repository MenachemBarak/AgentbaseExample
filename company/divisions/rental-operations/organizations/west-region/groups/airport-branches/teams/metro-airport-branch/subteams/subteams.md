# Subteams of the Metro Airport Branch

The **Metro Airport Branch** (Airport Branches Group, West Region Operations)
splits its on-the-ground work into three subteams, each owning a distinct stretch
of the rental lifecycle (reserve -> pickup -> in-use -> return -> invoice).
Together they keep vehicles cycling cleanly from the return lot back out to the
next customer, under Branch Manager **Aisha Khan**.

## The subteams

### Rental Counter
The customer-facing front desk. Checks customers in and out: pulls reservations,
verifies license and payment, prices upgrades, and issues keys for the
**pickup**.
- People: **Grace Liu** (Counter Team Lead) over Associate **Ben Foster**.
- Boundary: owns the desk and the customer conversation; does not clean, fuel, or
  inspect vehicles. Requests staged cars from Vehicle Prep and routes returning
  customers to Returns & Inspection.

### Vehicle Prep
The behind-the-scenes lot crew. Cleans, fuels, charges, and stages vehicles by
class so a ready car is waiting the moment the counter completes a pickup.
- Boundary: owns vehicle readiness up to the point of handover; does not handle
  customers or payment. Feeds staged cars to the Rental Counter.

### Returns & Inspection
The drop-off crew. Receives returning vehicles, reads GPS telematics (mileage,
fuel, diagnostics), inspects for damage, and closes out the rental toward the
**invoice**.
- Boundary: owns the return and damage check; final charges pass through the
  branch's **conductor** workflow human-in-the-loop damage gate before the
  customer is billed. Bulky telematics and maintenance logs arrive
  **headroom**-compressed.

## How they fit together
Rental Counter sends customers out, Vehicle Prep keeps ready cars flowing to the
counter, and Returns & Inspection brings them back in — a continuous loop that
turns each returned vehicle into the next customer's pickup.

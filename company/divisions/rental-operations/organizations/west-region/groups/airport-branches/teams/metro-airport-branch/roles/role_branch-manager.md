# Role — Branch Manager (role_branch-manager)

## Definition
The **Branch Manager** leads a single DriveEasy branch end to end. At the **Metro
Airport Branch** this role is held by **Aisha Khan**. She sits at the top of the
branch, reporting up to the Airport Branches Group — Manager **Priya Patel** and
Operations Supervisor **Tom Becker** — and directing the branch's Rental Sales
Agents and its three subteams (Rental Counter, Vehicle Prep, Returns & Inspection).

## Responsibilities
- Own the branch's daily operations, results, and customer-satisfaction scores.
- Set staffing and shift coverage across the counter, prep, and returns subteams.
- Manage the on-hand fleet mix — requesting cars from Regional Fleet Coordinator
  **Sofia Marin** and flagging surplus or shortfalls early.
- Approve exceptions: pricing overrides, damage waivers, and escalated disputes.
- Coach the agents and uphold DriveEasy service standards on the floor.

## Purpose
Aisha exists to turn the branch's bookings into smooth, on-time rentals — the right
car ready for every arriving traveler and a clean, fair return for every departing
one — translating the group's targets into a well-run airport counter.

# Role: Counter Team Lead (role_counter-lead)

## Definition
**Grace Liu** is the **Counter Team Lead** of the Rental Counter subteam at the
Metro Airport Branch (Airport Branches Group, West Region Operations, Rental
Operations division of DriveEasy Car Rental). She sits at the top of the Rental
Counter shift crew, directing the Rental Counter Associates and reporting to
Branch Manager **Aisha Khan**.

## Responsibilities
- Open, run, and close the front counter shift: rota, queue flow, breaks, and the
  cash/card till reconciliation.
- Handle escalations associates can't resolve — declined payments, license or ID
  problems, and upgrade or pricing disputes from the **pricing engine**.
- Keep pickup waits low during flight-arrival surges by balancing desk positions
  and calling for staged vehicles from Vehicle Prep.
- Maintain the shift log and surface staffing gaps, recurring issues, and
  fleet-class shortages to the Branch Manager.
- Own counter compliance: correct license verification, payment capture, and
  signed rental agreements.

## Purpose
Grace exists to make the front counter run smoothly and fairly every shift — so
that each customer reaches their vehicle quickly and every check-in and check-out
is accurate enough for the return and invoice stages to close without dispute.

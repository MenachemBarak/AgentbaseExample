# Roles — Metro Airport Branch

## Role hierarchy
Two roles run the floor at the Metro Airport Branch:

- **role_branch-manager** — **Aisha Khan**, Branch Manager. Accountable for the whole
  branch: results, fleet on hand, staffing, and customer experience.
- **role_rental-agent** — Rental Sales Agent (exemplar **Diego Alvarez**). Front-line
  staff who move customers through the rental lifecycle at the counter.

The Branch Manager sits above the Rental Sales Agents. Aisha reports up to the
Airport Branches Group — Manager **Priya Patel** and Operations Supervisor
**Tom Becker**. The agents report to Aisha and take day-to-day shift direction from
Counter Team Lead **Grace Liu** on the Rental Counter.

- [roles/role_branch-manager.md](roles/role_branch-manager.md)
- [roles/role_rental-agent.md](roles/role_rental-agent.md)

## Collaboration & communication
- **Daily huddle:** Aisha briefs the agents on arrivals, VIP bookings, and the day's
  fleet position from Sofia Marin before the first shuttle lands.
- **Counter handoffs:** agents pass in-progress rentals across shifts through the
  booking system and the shift-log, so nothing is dropped between shifts.
- **Escalation path:** pricing and damage disputes go agent -> Branch Manager ->
  Group Operations Supervisor; fleet shortfalls go Branch Manager -> Regional Fleet
  Coordinator.
- **Cross-subteam:** the counter coordinates with Vehicle Prep and Returns &
  Inspection so a car promised at the desk is actually clean, fueled, and ready on
  the lot.

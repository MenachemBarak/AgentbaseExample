# Airport Branches Group — Roles

## Role hierarchy
The Airport Branches Group is led by two roles:

- **role_group-manager** — Airport Branches Manager (**Priya Patel**)
  - **role_operations-supervisor** — Group Operations Supervisor (**Tom Becker**)

The group manager owns strategy, standards, and results for all airport branches
and reports up to West Regional Manager **James Carter**. The operations
supervisor reports to the group manager and owns day-to-day execution across the
branches.

See each role definition:
- [roles/role_group-manager.md](roles/role_group-manager.md)
- [roles/role_operations-supervisor.md](roles/role_operations-supervisor.md)

## Collaboration & communication
- **Priya -> Tom:** Priya sets weekly targets (utilization, wait time, upgrade
  rate); Tom translates them into staffing, shuttle, and counter coverage plans.
- **Upward:** Priya syncs with James Carter on regional goals and with Fleet
  Coordinator **Sofia Marin** on moving cars to where flights are landing.
- **Downward:** Tom works directly with each branch manager (e.g., **Aisha Khan**
  at the Metro Airport Branch) on shift coverage and exceptions.
- **Cadence:** a short daily ops huddle for live issues, a weekly group review of
  the numbers, and an immediate escalation path for vehicle shortages or shuttle
  delays during arrival peaks.
- Shared, deduped customer history keeps every airport counter speaking the same
  language about a given renter — no one re-asks what the system already knows.

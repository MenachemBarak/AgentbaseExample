# West Region Operations — Groups

West Region Operations organizes its branches into three groups by location type.
Each group runs the branches that share an environment and a customer rhythm, so
standards and staffing fit how those customers actually arrive. All three groups
report to West Regional Manager **James Carter** and draw vehicles from the same
regional pool balanced by Fleet Coordinator **Sofia Marin**.

## Airport Branches Group
Runs every West branch located at an airport. Flight-driven demand, terminal
shuttles, and a fast counter define its work. Led by Airport Branches Manager
**Priya Patel** with Group Operations Supervisor **Tom Becker**.
See [airport-branches/group.md](airport-branches/group.md).

## City Branches Group
Runs the downtown and city-center branches. Walk-in and business renters, garage
parking, and street-level counters shape its day rather than flight schedules.

## Suburban Branches Group
Runs the neighborhood and suburban branches. Steady local demand, weekly and
weekend renters, and easy lot access define this group, with lighter peaks than
the airports.

## Boundaries & relationships
- **By location, not by customer:** a renter belongs to whichever branch serves
  them; the groups divide branches by setting (airport / city / suburban), not by
  splitting customers.
- **Shared regional resources:** all groups use one booking system, one pricing
  engine, and one regional fleet — Sofia Marin moves vehicles between groups as
  demand shifts (e.g., feeding cars to the airports on a heavy arrival day).
- **Peer escalation:** groups lend each other vehicles, and occasionally staff,
  through James Carter rather than negotiating supply one-on-one.
- **Consistent experience:** vehicle classes, the rental lifecycle, and DriveEasy
  service standards are identical across groups; only the on-the-ground rhythm
  differs.

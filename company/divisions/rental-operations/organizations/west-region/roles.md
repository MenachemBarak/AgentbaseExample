# West Region Operations — Roles

## Role hierarchy
West Region Operations is led by a regional manager who owns the region's results,
supported by a fleet coordinator who keeps vehicles balanced across branches.

- **role_regional-manager** — [James Carter](roles/role_regional-manager.md), West Regional Manager
  - Owns the whole organization; reports up to the Director of Regional Operations (Dana Okonkwo).
  - **role_fleet-coordinator** — [Sofia Marin](roles/role_fleet-coordinator.md), Regional Fleet Coordinator
    - Reports to the regional manager; coordinates vehicles across all West groups and branches.

## Collaboration & communication
- **Top-down:** James sets regional targets and priorities; Sofia translates them into
  daily vehicle moves and stocking plans for each group.
- **Bottom-up:** Group managers (e.g., Priya Patel of the Airport Branches Group) raise
  shortages, surges, and staffing needs to James; Sofia acts on the fleet side.
- **Sideways:** Sofia coordinates with the Fleet Management division and neighboring
  regions on vehicle transfers, recalls, and one-way rentals.
- **Cadence:** a weekly regional review (utilization, revenue, service scores) plus a
  daily fleet stand-up; urgent shortages are handled live through the booking system.

# Rental Operations — Organizations

The **Rental Operations** division runs DriveEasy Car Rental's branches through three
regional organizations. Each region owns every branch within its geography and reports to
**Dana Okonkwo**, Director of Regional Operations. Together they cover the national footprint
with no overlap.

## Organizations in this division

### West Region Operations  *(fully detailed here)*
Runs all DriveEasy branches across the western states. Led by **James Carter**
(West Regional Manager) with **Sofia Marin** (Regional Fleet Coordinator). Organized into the
Airport, City, and Suburban Branches Groups. See [west-region/organization.md](west-region/organization.md).

### East Region Operations
Runs all branches across the eastern states, including the busiest coastal airport hubs.
Mirrors the West's structure with its own regional manager and fleet coordinator.

### Central Region Operations
Runs all branches across the central states, with heavy one-way and cross-country traffic.
Also mirrors the West's group structure.

## Boundaries & relationships
- **Geographic split:** each region owns its own states and branches; a branch belongs to
  exactly one region. Customers crossing regions (one-way rentals) are handed off via the
  shared booking system.
- **Fleet between regions:** vehicle transfers across regional lines are coordinated by the
  regions' fleet coordinators together with the Fleet Management division.
- **Common rails:** all three regions use the same booking system, pricing engine, and
  rental lifecycle (reserve -> pickup -> in-use -> return -> invoice), so standards stay consistent.
- **Escalation:** region-wide issues roll up to Dana Okonkwo and, when needed, to
  **Marcus Hale**, VP of Rental Operations.

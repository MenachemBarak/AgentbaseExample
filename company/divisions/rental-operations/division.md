# Rental Operations Division

## Overview
Rental Operations is the division of **DriveEasy Car Rental** that runs every
branch and counter — the people, processes, and systems that turn a reservation
into a car on the road and back again as a smooth return. It is the company's
largest, most customer-facing division and reports directly to CEO **Elena
Reyes**.

Rental Operations is one of four divisions, alongside **Fleet Management**
(supplies and maintains the cars), **Customer Experience** (brand, loyalty, and
support), and **Finance & Administration** (pricing approvals, billing, and the
back office).

## How it works
The division is organized by geography. It is split into regional
**organizations** — **West Region Operations**, **East Region Operations**, and
**Central Region Operations** — each of which runs groups of branches (airport,
city, suburban) down to individual counters.

- **Marcus Hale**, VP of Rental Operations, owns the division: operating
  standards, staffing models, and performance targets for every branch.
- **Dana Okonkwo**, Director of Regional Operations, oversees the regions, keeps
  them consistent, and coaches the regional managers who run them.

Day to day the division executes the rental lifecycle — **reserve -> pickup ->
in-use -> return -> invoice** — using the booking system, counter terminals, and
pricing engine, while coordinating with Fleet Management for vehicle supply and
Finance & Administration for pricing and billing.

## Purpose & goals
- Deliver the rental lifecycle reliably and consistently at every branch.
- Keep counters staffed and wait times short, vehicles ready at pickup, and
  returns fast and accurate.
- Hit fleet-utilization, revenue, and customer-satisfaction targets across all
  regions and every vehicle class (economy, compact, SUV, luxury, van).

## Vision
Every DriveEasy branch — from a busy airport counter to a quiet suburban lot —
feels like the same effortless, well-run place to pick up a car.

## Mission
Run branch operations so smoothly that every customer is on the road quickly in
a reliable, fairly-priced vehicle, served by friendly, well-supported staff.

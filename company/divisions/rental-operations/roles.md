# Rental Operations — Roles

This division is led by two roles that set direction and keep every region
running to the same standard. Vision & mission live in
[division.md](division.md); this file covers the roles, their hierarchy, and how
they work together.

## Roles hierarchy
- **role_operations-vp** — **Marcus Hale**, VP of Rental Operations. The most
  senior operations role; owns all branch operations and reports to CEO **Elena
  Reyes**.
  - **role_regional-director** — **Dana Okonkwo**, Director of Regional
    Operations. Reports to Marcus Hale and oversees the three regional
    organizations and their regional managers.

See:
- [roles/role_operations-vp.md](roles/role_operations-vp.md)
- [roles/role_regional-director.md](roles/role_regional-director.md)

## Collaboration & communication
- **Cadence.** Marcus and Dana hold a weekly operations review covering
  utilization, wait times, staffing, and customer-satisfaction scores by region.
  Dana runs a separate weekly call with the regional managers and rolls findings
  up to Marcus.
- **Division of labor.** Marcus sets the standards, targets, and budget and
  represents Rental Operations to the CEO and peer divisions. Dana translates
  those standards into regional execution and surfaces on-the-ground issues back
  up.
- **Cross-division.** Together they coordinate with Fleet Management on vehicle
  supply, with Finance & Administration on pricing and billing, and with
  Customer Experience on service standards.
- **Escalation.** Branch and regional problems flow up through Dana; anything
  affecting company strategy, budget, or cross-division commitments Marcus takes
  to Elena Reyes.
